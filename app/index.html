Make your travel reimbursements less painful.

Design Questions

  • How can we help companies track and reduce their travel budget?
  • How can we make the travel reimbursement process smooth and easy?

Secondary Research

I began with secondary research by comparing different web and mobile solutions that are trying to solve this problem.

This gave me a good understand of what the current solutions and also enabled me to list down the common features.

Questions unanswered

  • Who approves the bills?
  • What is the organization structure?
  • How many levels of Approvals?
  • Who allocates the travel budget?
  • What happens if the user goes over budget?
  • What can be the potential expense categories?
  • Do we need to support different currency types?
  • How account manager can take action?
  • What kind of actions are these?

Design Requirements

Make sure that travel Expenses are with in budget.
Review and approve travel claims with ease.
Track own travel expenses and help reduce expenses.
Reduce travel expenses and increase savings.

Ideation

I sketched multiple different ideas that could solve the problem.

“What if...?”

  • Adding bills were simple and faster.
  • Your app could tell you when employee forget to add a bill.
  • A dashboard can tell a story of your company travel expenses.
  • Manage all your travel and reimbursement requests in a single page.

Mobile technologies offer new opportunities for the employees to track their expenses on the go.
